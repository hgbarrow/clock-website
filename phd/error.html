About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering PhD candidate conducting research at the University of California in Berkeley. More specifically, I focus on the design, fabrication, and characterization of MEMS with the aim of enhancing the performance of several of the discrete components found in your cell phone today. At UC Berkeley I also enjoy sharing my knowledge as a teaching assistant for courses in MEMS, microfabrication, and analog circuit design.
